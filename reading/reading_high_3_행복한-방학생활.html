📖 주제: 행복한 방학생활
🎓 학교급: elementary 3학년
📅 생성일: 2025. 8. 21.

📖 영어 지문

Summer vacation is always a happy time for every kid. No school, no homework, and all day just for fun! One sunny afternoon, a boy named Jack decided to enjoy his vacation at the beach. He woke up early in the morning, ate his breakfast, and packed his bag. His bag was full of fun things: a ball, a book, sunscreen, and a bottle of water. Jack's dad drove him to the beach. As they arrived, Jack felt the warm air and the cool blue water across his face. He could also smell the salty sea. At the beach, Jack found a large space to lay down his towel. He took off his shoes and let his feet feel the soft, warm sand. He then grabbed his ball and started to play catch with his dad. Jack's dad was good at catching the ball. They laughed and had a great time under the bright sun. After a while, Jack decided to build a sandcastle. He enjoyed shaping the sand into towers and walls. It was not easy, but he was happy to see his castle slowly take shape. He noticed a small bird flying around his castle. It was a beautiful sight. Soon, Jack felt hungry. He sat down and opened his bag. Inside, he found a sandwich his mom had packed for him. It tasted even better at the beach. He also drank the water from his bottle. It felt refreshing. As the sun began to set, Jack and his dad decided to go home. Jack was tired but he felt happy. He had a wonderful day at the beach. He thought about how lucky he was to have such a fun day. On the way home, Jack looked out of the car window. He saw a beautiful sunset. The sky was full of orange and purple colors. He felt grateful for such a beautiful day. He couldn't wait for another exciting day of his summer vacation. For Jack, this was a perfect summer day. He learned that happiness can be found in simple things: playing a game, building a sandcastle, eating a good sandwich, and spending time with family. This is what made his summer vacation so special. He couldn't wait for what the next day would bring.
