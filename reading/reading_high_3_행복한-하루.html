📖 영어 지문

Once upon a time, in a large city, there lived a young boy named Tom. Tom had a big family. His dad and mom, his brother and sister, and his kind aunt all lived in a big house. They also had a cat and a dog. Their home was always full of love and laughter. One sunny afternoon, Tom decided to have a happy day with his family. He woke up early, right after his dad left for work, and his mom began to cook breakfast in the kitchen. The smell of bread and eggs filled the house. Tom's sister, who had golden hair like the morning sun, helped their mom. Tom's brother, who was older and taller, was busy reading a book in the corner. After breakfast, Tom and his family went to the beach. The sky was blue, and the air was fresh. They played with a ball and also built a sandcastle. Tom's aunt took many pictures with her camera. The sun was high in the sky, and the sound of the waves was like music to their ears. In the afternoon, they went to the park. Tom's brother brought a football, and they played an exciting game. Tom's sister picked flowers, and their mom made a beautiful flower crown for her. Their dog ran around, chasing a butterfly, and their cat was relaxing under a tree. In the evening, they returned home. Tom's dad cooked dinner. He was a good cook. They sat around the dining table, talked about their day, and laughed a lot. After dinner, they gathered in the living room. Tom's aunt told a funny story, and they all laughed until their bellies hurt. As the day came to an end, Tom thought about his day. He realized that it's the simple things, like spending time with family and enjoying the beauty around, that make a day happy. He thanked God for his family and promised to enjoy every day as if it was a happy day. This is what a happy day looked like for Tom and his family. How about your happy day? What does it look like? You see, a happy day can be any day when you are with the people you love, doing what you love. So, enjoy every day, because every day can be a happy day!
